Student Health Services

Take care of yourself

At Florida Polytechnic University, we believe that an active and healthy body is essential to a successful learning environment. Our facilities include:

  • Fitness Center with state-of-the-art cardio and strength training equipment
  • Two lighted basketball courts and a beach volleyball court
  • Multi-purpose field for flag football, softball, lacrosse, Frisbee and more
  • Health Clinic for any physical, psychological and developmental concerns

For more information on Florida Poly’s Student Health Services, visit our website.
