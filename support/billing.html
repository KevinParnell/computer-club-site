Tuition & Fees

Information about billing and the payment process are outlined below.

Tuition & Fee Statements

Once you have made your deposit and submitted all your required forms, you can access your Personal Account Ledger 24/7 by logging into the Florida Poly Pulse or contacting the Bursar’s Office during regular business hours. Your Ledger will include all tuition and fee charges and payments associated with your account. We encourage you to review your Ledger statement prior to payment, once after initial registration, and any time changes are made to your initial registration, to ensure that fees are adjusted accordingly. Statements are not mailed.

Tuition and fees must be paid by the published dates. The University reserves the right to assess late fees and cancel classes for any tuition and fees not paid by the payment deadlines as specified in the Academic Calendar,

Tuition & Fee Payments

Payments must be received or postmarked no later than the fee payment deadlines, as specified on the Academic Calendar. Payments may be submitted by mail, paid in person at the Bursar’s Office, or made online. Payments cannot be accepted by telephone.

Acceptable Forms of Payment

  • Personal Check
  • Credit Card
  • Online

Online Payments

Sign in online and then click on the link for CAMS. Select Administrative Services, My Ledger to access your account statement and pay your bill.

  • Online credit card payments will be subject to a non-refundable 2.3% convenience fee per transaction.
  • Payments made at any time prior to or on the date of the published fee payment deadline will be considered on time.

Mailed Payments

  • Include the student’s name and student ID# on checks or money orders (payable to “Florida Polytechnic University”)
  • Address payments to:
    Florida Polytechnic University
    Bursar’s Office
    Attn: Payment Processing
    4700 Research Way, Room 1103
    Lakeland, FL 33805-8531
