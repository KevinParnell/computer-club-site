Checklist for Admitted Students

Here’s an easy way to keep track of everything you need to do before arriving on the Florida Polytechnic University campus. As you approach the end of your senior year and head into summer, don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions.

First, congratulate yourself on being accepted to Florida's newest and most innovative university, Florida Poly!

Next, check off these important steps as you prepare for arrival at Florida Poly.

  • Please pay your deposit as soon as possible here to secure your seat in the Fall 2017 class.
  • Reserve your spot in the residence hall. On-campus housing is in high demand–the sooner you apply, the better!
  • Complete the Residency Classification Application.

    If you have forgotten your login, please contact admissions at admissions@flpoly.org.

  • Send in your medical forms. Make an appointment for a physical exam soon, and have your doctor fill out and sign:
    • Medical history form
    • Mandatory immunization form
  • Request your final transcript. When your grades are final, ask your guidance office to send us your final transcript.
  • Explore majors. Familiarize yourself with degree programs and concentrations listed on our website.
  • Like us on Facebook. Follow Florida Poly's Facebook page for updates and news about STEM innovation.
  • Start packing! Move–in days are August 15 and 16.
