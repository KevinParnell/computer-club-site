Resident Hall Information

Live large. Learn large.

We want to make Florida Poly your home away from home.

Both on-campus residence halls were designed to provide comfortable, safe housing just steps from the main
classroom building.

Residence Hall I

This 90,000-square-foot residential building features fully furnished three- and four-bedroom suites and state-of-the-art technology. Amenities include:

  • Full-size beds, desks and dressers
  • High-speed, wireless Internet access
  • Fitness facility and entertainment room
  • In-suite washers and dryers
  • Electronic access and security cameras
  • Computer center, study lounge and large common area

Residence Hall 2

Semi suites consisting of two double bedrooms furnished with two twin beds, dressers, and desks with chairs make up the majority of this five-floor, 135,000-square-foot facility. Other amenities include:

  • High-speed, wireless Internet access
  • Entertainment center and game rooms
  • Computer center and fitness facility
  • In-suite washers and dryers
  • Large common areas to encourage to social networking
  • Electronic access and security cameras
