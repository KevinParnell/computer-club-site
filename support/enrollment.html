You’re In! What’s Next?

Congratulations on your acceptance to Florida Polytechnic University. We’re looking forward to welcoming you to campus soon.

Follow the steps below to confirm your place in our next class of trailblazers!

Step 1: Make Your Deposit

An admission deposit of $200 is required to confirm your enrollment at Florida Poly. Your deposit will be credited directly to your tuition account and is non-refundable.

Final confirmation of admission is contingent upon receipt of your deposit, which can be submitted here. Deposits must be received by:

  • December 1 for students enrolling in the Spring 2017 semester
  • May 1 for students enrolling in the Fall 2017 semester

Step 2: Housing Preference

On-campus housing is in high demand, so don't delay! Make your deposit and then reserve your place in the residence hall. You can learn about your room options, residence hall amenities, housing costs, and more on our website. Simply click "apply" to request your reservation.

Step 3: Final Transcript

Please send your official high school transcript with final grades and notation of high school graduation by July 1, 2017. Remember that satisfactory completion of your final term is required in order to enroll. Address transcripts to:

Florida Polytechnic University
Office of Admissions
4700 Research Way
Lakeland, FL 33805-9531

Step 4: Financial Aid

As an admitted student, you will be receiving a packet from the Office of Financial Aid containing the following information:

  • Our Financial Aid Brochure for new students and their families
  • An estimated award letter outlining possible awards for the upcoming year
  • Your Florida Poly Scholarship award amount

Estimating your costs: We encourage you to try out the Net Price Calculator in the financial aid section of our website. The Net Price Calculator will help you determine your possible costs and financial aid during your first year as a Florida Poly student.

Financial aid questions? Email us directly at financialaid@FLPoly.org or call 800-585-7659.

Step 5: Physical Exam

All incoming students must submit a medical health history form, including mandatory immunizations, completed and signed by your doctor. Getting an appointment with your physician can take time, so please schedule your physical as soon as possible. Click here for more info on Florida Poly’s Health Clinic.

Step 6: Motor Vehicles

Permits are required for vehicles parked on campus. As a student, you’ll pay an annual fee of $115 for a parking permit or a fee of $60 for a single semester. Visit our website for instructions on how to get a parking permit.

Step 7: Registration

Your Admissions Counselor will notify you about steps and dates to register for classes.

Step 8: Transferable Credits

You may earn credit toward a Florida Poly degree through several credit-by-examination programs, under the following guidelines:

  • You must take the exams (AICE, AP, IB CLEP) and report your scores to the university before enrolling, or, at the latest, before the end of your first term.
  • You may earn a maximum of 45 semester hours by combining AICE, AP, IB, and CLEP credits.

Equivalent courses earned by examination generally fulfill the same requirements that a Florida Polytechnic University course fulfills. Credit will only be awarded once for any subject.
