Parking & Transportation

Get from here to there

Whether you live in the residence hall or commute to campus, you are welcome to bring your vehicle—you just need to obtain a parking permit from our Parking and Transportation Office. Students may park in one of two lots designated for general permit parking.

Student Parking Permit Fees:

  • Annual: $115
  • One semester: $60

Student parking permit instructions are on our website.

Visiting campus?  There are three lots designated as Visitor Park & Pay. Find a map of our campus, including designated parking lots, on our website.

Florida Poly also offers complimentary bus transportation to select local destinations. Before you arrive on campus, check out Parking and Transportation on our website, contact the Parking and Transportation Office at parking@FLPoly.org, or call 863-874-8440.
