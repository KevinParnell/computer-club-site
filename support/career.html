Career Services

Take the next step

At Florida Polytechnic University, we’re serious about being the “jobs” university. So we begin preparing you for what’s next on day one. When you graduate with a Florida Poly degree, you’ll move forward with industry connections and experience in your field – and a clear path to career success.

Career Services Center

Our mission at the Career Services Center is to prepare you for leadership positions in the dynamic, fast-growing technological landscape in Florida, the nation, and the world.

We work closely with leading-edge companies and organizations in our region to provide a robust offering of mentorships, internships, co-ops, and job opportunities, which are available to you both as a student and later on as a Florida Poly graduate. Our Industry Partners provide opportunities for real-world experience and hands-on research that can turn into jobs before you even graduate.

To help you get the most from these valuable opportunities, Career Services offers tools, programs, and resources to help guide you through each step of your career journey. Technological and traditional services are available, including:

  • Career counseling
  • Resume preparation
  • Career meet-ups
  • Workshops on topics such as how to market your brand
  • Access to internship and job opportunities on Purple Briefcase

Learn more about our Industry Partners and the opportunities for collaboration and research that we make possible for our students.
